## Community Guidelines

1. **Stay respectful** - professional discourse keeps the signal high
2. **Protect sensitive data** - never share private keys or seed phrases in public forums
3. **Flag incidents quickly** - report suspected bugs to [security@layercover.com](mailto:security@layercover.com) before posting publicly
4. **Keep it constructive** - cite data, dashboards, or on-chain evidence when debating
