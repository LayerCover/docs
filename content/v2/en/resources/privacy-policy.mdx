## 1. Introduction

LayerCover Labs Ltd ("**LayerCover**," "**we**," "**us**") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information when you use the LayerCover protocol, website, documentation, SDK, and associated services (collectively, the "**Services**").

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Wallet Addresses** - When you connect a wallet to interact with the protocol
- **Contact Information** - If you voluntarily contact us via email (e.g., [legal@layercover.com](mailto:legal@layercover.com) or [security@layercover.com](mailto:security@layercover.com))
- **Referral Data** - If you participate in the [Referral System](/governance/referral-system), referral relationships are recorded on-chain

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage Data** - Pages visited, features used, time spent, and interaction patterns on our website and documentation
- **Device Information** - Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and language preferences
- **Network Information** - IP address, approximate geographic location, and Internet Service Provider
- **Blockchain Data** - Transaction hashes, smart contract interactions, and on-chain event data (this data is inherently public on the blockchain)

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Wallet Providers** - Connection metadata from wallet services (e.g., WalletConnect, MetaMask)
- **Analytics Providers** - Aggregated usage metrics from tools such as Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or similar services
- **On-Ramp Partners** - If you use integrated fiat on-ramp services (e.g., MoonPay), those providers share limited transaction status data with us

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

| Purpose | Legal Basis |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Operate the Services** - Display your positions, policies, and syndicate data | Contractual necessity |
| **Improve the Services** - Analyse usage patterns to improve UX and performance | Legitimate interest |
| **Security & Fraud Prevention** - Detect suspicious activity and enforce access controls | Legitimate interest |
| **Compliance** - Enforce [Restricted Territory](/resources/terms-of-service#10-restricted-territories) restrictions and sanctions screening | Legal obligation |
| **Communications** - Respond to your enquiries and provide support | Contractual necessity |
| **Analytics** - Understand aggregate trends in protocol usage | Legitimate interest |

## 4. On-Chain Data

<Callout type="warning">
  **Blockchain transactions are permanently public.** Any data you submit to the blockchain - including wallet addresses, transaction amounts, policy purchases, claims, and syndicate interactions - is visible to anyone and **cannot be deleted or modified** by LayerCover or any other party.
</Callout>

We do not control the blockchain. On-chain data is not subject to deletion requests and exists independently of our Services.

---

## 5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

- **Essential Cookies** - Maintain session state and wallet connections
- **Analytics Cookies** - Measure site traffic and feature adoption
- **Preference Cookies** - Remember your display settings (e.g., dark mode, preferred network)

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may impair the functionality of the Services.

We do **not** sell your personal information. We may share data with:

## 7. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Usage & Analytics Data** | 24 months from collection, then anonymised or deleted |
| **Contact & Support Data** | Duration of the enquiry plus 12 months |
| **Wallet Addresses** (off-chain records) | Duration of active use plus 12 months |
| **On-Chain Data** | Permanent (immutable blockchain record, outside our control) |

## 8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

- Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
- Access controls and principle of least privilege for internal systems
- Regular security assessments and monitoring
- No storage of private keys, seed phrases, or wallet credentials

Despite these measures, no system is completely secure. You are responsible for safeguarding your wallet credentials and private keys.

---

## 9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

1. **Access** - Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
2. **Rectification** - Request correction of inaccurate personal data
3. **Erasure** - Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements and the limitations of on-chain data)
4. **Restriction** - Request that we limit processing of your personal data
5. **Portability** - Receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format
6. **Objection** - Object to processing based on legitimate interests
7. **Withdraw Consent** - Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time

To exercise these rights, contact [privacy@layercover.com](mailto:privacy@layercover.com). We will respond within **30 days** of receiving your request.

---

## 10. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries outside your jurisdiction, including the United Kingdom and European Economic Area. Where data is transferred internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions.

## 11. Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed to individuals under the age of **18**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact [privacy@layercover.com](mailto:privacy@layercover.com) and we will take steps to delete it.
