LayerCover prioritizes security through a multi-layered approach combining formal audits, economic modeling, and a staged mainnet rollout.

## Guarded Mainnet Launch

To mitigate early-stage risk, LayerCover will execute a **staged mainnet rollout** (similar to EigenLayer).

* **Deposit Caps:** Initial liquidity will be strictly limited via global TVL caps and per-pool maximums.
* **Progressive Expansion:** Caps will be raised incrementally via governance only after stability milestones are met.
* **Objective:** This ensures the protocol scale matches its proven security maturity, preventing outsized losses during the hardening phase.

## Bug Bounty Program

**Launch: Q4 2026**

LayerCover will launch a **$1,000,000** bug bounty program hosted on **Immunefi**.

* **Scope:** All production smart contracts, SDKs, APIs, and frontends.
* **Payouts:** Tiered based on severity (OWASP/Impact).
* **Competition:** The program opens with a time-bound competition offering bonus multipliers and spotlight features for top-ranked pre-launch discoveries.
