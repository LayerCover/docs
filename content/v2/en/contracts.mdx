This section provides detailed reference documentation for the core smart contracts and interfaces of the LayerCover protocol.

## Core Vaults

*   [**ISyndicate**](/contracts/ISyndicate): The main underwriting vault interface.
*   [**ICapitalPool**](/contracts/ICapitalPool): The underlying asset storage.
*   [**ISharedAssetController**](/contracts/ISharedAssetController): The Reinsurance (Backstop) system.

## Policy & Risk

*   [**IPolicyManager**](/contracts/IPolicyManager): Policy issuance and lifecycle.
*   [**IPayoutManager**](/contracts/IPayoutManager): Claim payout execution.
*   [**IRiskManager**](/contracts/IRiskManager): Risk assessment and incident reporting.

## Management & Registry

*   [**IUnderwriterManager**](/contracts/IUnderwriterManager): Capital allocation and intent management.
*   [**IPoolRegistry**](/contracts/IPoolRegistry): Central registry for underwriting pools.
*   [**ISyndicateFactory**](/contracts/ISyndicateFactory): Deployment factory for Syndicates.

## Yield & Liquidity

*   [**IYieldStrategyManager**](/contracts/IYieldStrategyManager): Manages external yield strategies.
*   [**IYieldAdapter**](/contracts/IYieldAdapter): Interface for integrating new yield sources (e.g., Aave, Compound).
*   [**IWithdrawalQueue**](/contracts/IWithdrawalQueue): Handles the two-step withdrawal process.

## Assets & Pricing

*   [**IPolicyNFT**](/contracts/IPolicyNFT): The tokenized insurance policy (ERC721).
*   [**IRateEngine**](/contracts/IRateEngine): Dynamic pricing logic and risk models.

## Intents & Rewards

*   [**IIntentMatcher**](/contracts/IIntentMatcher): Order book and matching for intent-based coverage.
*   [**IReferralManager**](/contracts/IReferralManager): Referral tracking and rewards.
*   [**IRewardDistributor**](/contracts/IRewardDistributor): Distribution of protocol rewards.
