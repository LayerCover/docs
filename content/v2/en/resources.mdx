Essential references and resources for working with LayerCover protocol.

## Quick Reference

These pages provide at-a-glance information for common needs:

### [Security & Audits](/resources/audits)
Professional security audits, testing practices, and bug bounty program. Review our security approach and audit reports.

### [Protocol Risks](/concepts/underwriting/risk-assessment)
Comprehensive risk disclosure for policyholders, underwriters, and liquidity providers. **Read before depositing funds.**

### [Contract Addresses](/resources/contract-addresses)
Deployed smart contract addresses across all supported networks. Essential for integration and verification.

### [Pool Parameters](/resources/parameters)
Current configuration and risk parameters for all insurance pools, including coverage terms, capital allocation, and risk ratings.

### [Access Controls](/resources/access-controls)
Role-based permission system, multi-sig requirements, and timelock mechanisms protecting the protocol.

### [Code Licensing](/resources/licensing)
Software licenses (BUSL, MIT, CC BY) and usage terms for smart contracts, SDK, and documentation.

### [Brand Kit](/resources/brand-kit)
Official logos, colors, typography, and brand guidelines. Download assets and review usage policies.
